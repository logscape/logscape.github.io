Getting Started/ Welcome to Logscape

Welcome.

If you're here then you've taken the first step towards making use of your infrastructures logs. And the question now, is what happens next?

Nowadays, your business runs on mobile devices, applications, network devices and servers that are all generating large amounts of data, when things go wrong it can be hard to fix issues quickly, since diagnosis of the problem is so difficult, that's where Logscape comes in. Logscape Cloud is a log aggregration and analysis tool, that grows as you do, designed to monitor the entirety of your infrastructure. Getting started is simple.

  • If you haven't already, then register your account on the Logscape Website.
  • Take a read of our getting started page, it will explain the basics.
  • Configure your devices, and you're good to go.


    • Infrastructure

      Logscape is capable of managing all of your company infrastructure, from Linux and windows machines, to networking hardware.

    Application Logs

      Have control of your own logs? Logscape understands industry standard logging conventions.

    Instrumentation

      With the aid of Syslog, Logscape can monitor a whole multitude of custom applications.


    Getting your data into the Cloud

    The easiest method for getting data into the Cloud is to set up data streaming through Syslog, or an equivalent on your OS - You also have the option of manually uploading logs via our website, most systems have a native method for getting data into Logscape, for those that do not there is the Logscape collector, the collector is a lightweight daemon that when installed on your machine is capable of running Logscape plugins to gather metrics about your system, and then upload these to the Logscape cloud.
