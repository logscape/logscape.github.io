Network Devices

Network device logs can be forwarded to the Logscape cloud after going through a centralized Syslog server. Simply attach your Logscape token and tag. Below you can find some guides for configuring your network devices. The ability to gather logs from your network devices allows you to monitor metrics such as firewall activity, and switch performance.
