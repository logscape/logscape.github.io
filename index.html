    What is Logscape

    Logscape is an Enterprise ready big-data analytics tool built for time-series data visualization of machine data. Whether it is data generated by your systems or extracted from an external source - any form of machine generated data can easily be Searched, Filtered and placed onto Interactive Workspaces.
