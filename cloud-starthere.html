Getting started with Logscape in the Cloud

New to Logscape? Well you're in the right place.

What is Logscape?

Logscape is a log aggregration and visualisation service, pipe all of your companies logs into the cloud and watch in amazement as Logscape seamlessly visualizes your searches, and reveals trends that you never even knew were there. Even better, with Logscape Cloud there are no hardware fees, and no special software required, if you can get your data to us, we'll do the rest.

What logs does Logscape support?

Logscape can be configured to import any text based log, and comes with out of box support for many platforms.

So how do I get data into Logscape Cloud?

You can either set up seamless data streaming through R-Syslog, Syslog-NG and HTTP or manually upload logs via our website, most systems have a native method for getting data into Logscape, for those that do not there is the Logscape collector, along with its wide selection of apps.
