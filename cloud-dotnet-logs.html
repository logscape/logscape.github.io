Stream your .Net logs with NLog

NLog is a free logging platform for .NET, Silverlight and Windows Phone with rich log routing and management capabilities. NLog makes it easy to produce and manage high-quality logs for your application regardless of its size or complexity.

Step 1) Download NLog

Nlog is a community driven logging library for .net, intended as a replacement for Apache's Log4Net, it is freely available from Nlog's download page. Once it has downloaded it's time to to move on.
