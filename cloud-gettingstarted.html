Introduction

Logscape is a SaaS panacea for your logging needs, capable of ingesting, and visualising, any plain text log file, if you can get your logs to us, we'll do the rest. Forget about hardware costs, don't worry about the thought of expansion, Logscape Cloud grows as you do. Has your project doubled in size? No problem. Best of all, Logscape Cloud doesn't need to be installed in your system, use a plethora of natively supported transport medians to get your data to the cloud.
